# About HeadySystems

HeadySystems Inc. was founded in Fort Collins, Colorado by systems architect Eric Haywood. The company emerged from a conviction that today's AI systems are too brittle: locked into single-model dependencies, boolean routing logic, and architectures that cannot introspect or heal themselves.

Heady™ is the answer — a living, self-organizing platform where intelligence emerges from the collective behavior of specialized agents, not from a single monolithic model.

HeadySystems operates alongside HeadyConnection Inc., a 501(c)(3) nonprofit focused on using AI to advance social equity and redistribute the benefits of automation to the communities that need them most.
